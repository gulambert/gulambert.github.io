Guerlain Lambert

PhD Student @ Centrale Lyon / Institut Camille Jordan

Hi ! I'm a third (and last 🤞🏻) year PhD student at Centrale Lyon in applied mathematics. I'm supervised by Céline Helbert (Centrale Lyon) and Claire Lauvernet (INRAE Lyon). I'm working on surrogate modeling of complex computer code in the context of Global Sensitivity Analysis.

Research Interests

My work primarily focuses on the following topics :

  • Metamodeling via Gaussian processes with complex data (dependent and functional data).
  • Sensitivity analysis (Sobol indices, DGSM, ...).
  • Bayesian optimization.
  • Application to environmental models (water ressources, pesticide transports).
